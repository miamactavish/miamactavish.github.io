King of Buggies

King of Buggies is a game created in a group of four for University of Calgary's CPSC585: Games Programming course in 2016. As per the requirements of the course, this game was created in C++, using OpenGL and Nvidia's PhysX library. No external engine was used; the engine was written from scratch by the group members.
